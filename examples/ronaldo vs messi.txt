# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Blog Post: Messi vs Ronaldo – The Greatest Football Debate Explained

### Target Audience

- Football fans  
- Sports analysts  
- Casual readers interested in the GOAT debate  
- Readers on Medium, LinkedIn, and tech/content blogs  

### Tone

Professional, analytical, neutral, and accessible.  
The article should be balanced, data-informed, and respectful to both sides.

### Length

900–1100 words

---

## Objective

Create a comprehensive, unbiased analysis of the long-standing football debate between Lionel Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo.  
The goal is not to declare a definitive winner, but to help readers understand why the debate exists, how both players differ, and why both are considered generational legends.

---

## Core Requirements

The blog post must:

1. Clearly define what the Messi vs Ronaldo debate is
2. Explain why comparisons between them are inevitable
3. Compare both players across multiple dimensions
4. Use real-world context, not fan-driven arguments
5. End without forcing a single “GOAT” conclusion

---

## Key Sections & Content Structure

### 1. Introduction: Why This Debate Exists

- Brief history of the rivalry
- Over a decade of simultaneous dominance
- Media and fan-driven polarization
- Why football has never seen a comparison like this before

---

### 2. Player Background Overview

Provide a concise overview of each player:

- Early career and development
- Club career trajectory
- Core footballing identity

Purpose: establish context before comparison.

---

### 3. Playing Style Comparison

Explain how they play, not just what they achieve.

Messi:

- Playmaking focus
- Dribbling, vision, and creativity
- System-based brilliance

Ronaldo:

- Physical and athletic dominance
- Goal-scoring instincts
- Adaptability across leagues and systems

Avoid subjective praise. Anchor points in observable match behavior.

---

### 4. Statistical Comparison (High-Level)

- Goals and assists (club + international)
- Consistency across seasons
- Performance in high-pressure matches

Statistics should support insights, not overwhelm the reader.

---

### 5. Trophies and Achievements

Compare:

- Domestic league titles
- International trophies
- Individual awards
- Records and milestones

Include context such as teams played for, leagues, and competition level.

---

### 6. International Career Impact

- Contributions to national teams
- Tournament performances
- Leadership roles
- Evolution of international success over time

Focus on narrative impact rather than just trophies.

---

### 7. Cultural and Global Influence

- Global fanbase and popularity
- Influence on younger players
- Commercial and brand presence
- Role in shaping modern football culture

This section explains why the debate goes beyond the pitch.

---

### 8. Why the Debate Has No Definitive Answer

- Different definitions of “greatness”
- Individual vs team-oriented metrics
- Style vs output
- Era, system, and role differences

This is the analytical core of the article.

---

### 9. Conclusion: Two Legends, One Era

- Summarize key contrasts
- Reinforce mutual greatness
- Emphasize appreciation over comparison
- Leave the final judgment to the reader

---

## Writing Guidelines

- No fanboy or troll language
- No declaring a clear winner
- Avoid absolutist statements
- Maintain logical flow and clean transitions
- Short paragraphs for readability

---

## Explicit Exclusions

- Memes or social media arguments
- Emotional or biased language
- Clickbait claims
- Declaring a definitive GOAT

---

## Deliverable

- One long-form blog post
- Markdown format
- SEO-friendly headings
- Neutral stance maintained throughout

---

## Success Criteria

The article is successful if:

- Fans of both players feel respected
- Readers gain clarity instead of conflict
- The debate is framed intellectually, not emotionally
